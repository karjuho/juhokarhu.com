Background

I was born in Finland, but grew up mostly in Sweden. Since I have always felt like a combination. So it was naturally to take a combined role when it was time to choose a profession. From the start, I was a designer and developer. I really like to combine the different parts, and to actually build the things I imagine. My coding experience helps me to not just understand the constrains, but also see the possibilities!

Currently I am super intersted in the topic of humanity centered design. Moving focus from the user to a more holistic perspective. How will this affect not only the user but other people, workers, sosciaty and enviroment? How can we make design more inclusive?

On my free time I love spending time with my family, video games and baking artisan pizza and bread.
