About

About me

I am a Product Designer born in Finland and raised in Sweden. When I started to learn design it was natural for me to also learn how to code as just imagining things was nice, but the real magic happened when I could build the designs to life as well! I had found my passion! I think my coding skills help me when I design to understand the constraints and see the possibilities.

In my free time, I love spending time with my family, board & video games and baking (hopefully) lovely smelling artisan pizza & bread.

Currently I study Design Lead at Hyper Island and learn about UX, team development, creativity and self leadership.
